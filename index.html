Background

With a hacker mindset since childhood, I have spent nearly 40-years learning how things work by decompiling them. From synthesizers to computers to the internet. I built my first pre-internet BBS affectionately called the Ice House (feat. Iceman the SysOp) in my parents basement during a grey winter in Canada. As the internet hit, I ran through all things networking, web design, zine publishing and online businesses (shout out to developing one of the first ever vlogs on iTunes). Fast forward to the last decade and I have spent countless hours working with startups and leading teams of 50+ to bring well designed products and technologies into the world. From finance to brand, factory floor to board room, I have developed a unique set of chops to drive technology and innovation forward.
