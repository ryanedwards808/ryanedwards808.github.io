Background

With a hacker mindset since childhood, I have spent nearly 40-years learning how things work by decompiling them. From synthesizers to computers to the internet. I built my first pre-internet BBS affectionately called the Ice House in my parents basement during a grey winter in Canada. As the internet hit I ran through all things cyber, from coding to networking, zine publishing to online businesses. Fast forward to the last decade and I have spent countless hours working with startups and leading teams of 50+ to bring design-led products and useful technologies into the world. From finance to brand, factory floor to board room, I have developed a unique set of chops to drive technology and innovation forward.
